# 🛡️ Lumina Daily: Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-04-02

## 1. Introduction
'Lumina Daily' (the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## 2. Information Collection
- **Personal Data:** We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or phone number) directly through the App.
- **AI Generation:** When you generate quotes or images, requests are processed using Google Gemini AI. These requests do not contain personal identifiers.
- **Firebase:** We use Firebase Firestore to store and display shared wisdom. Data stored is limited to the wisdom content and is not linked to your identity.

## 3. Data Usage
The information we collect is used solely to:
- Provide the core functionality of the App (AI Wisdom generation).
- Display shared quotes on the dashboard.
- Improve the user experience and App performance.

## 4. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data stored in Firebase. However, please be aware that no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services which may have their own privacy policies:
- **Google Gemini AI API** (for wisdom generation)
- **Firebase/Google Cloud** (for data storage)
- **Expo** (for application framework)

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the developer contact info in the Google Play Store.
